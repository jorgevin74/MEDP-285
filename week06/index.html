Brief summary of kendrick Lamar

Kendrick Lamar is an American Rapper born in Compton, California. You may have heard of him recently, especially because of last year's feud with Drake. He is also a staple in American Hip-Hop, having influences from earlier West Coast Rap songs present in his albums and eventually evolving his sound to become his own. This website gives you a chance to get to know who Kendrick Lamar is, his history, the awards he’s won, and a gallery showcasing him.

On the left-hand side, you’ll be greeted with a nav bar where each tab will take you to a different part of the website where you can learn all about who he is in the biography section, a look at his accolades and accomplishments in the awards section and lastly a small gallery that lets you take a look at him over the years of his career. Then on the right-hand side, you’ll get a chance to get to know his discography, to listen to each of his studio albums with links directing you to YouTube, and can listen to them in full. With this, you’ll get a chance to get to know who he is and experience the albums he has released throughout his discography.
