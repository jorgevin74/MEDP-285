Kendrick Lamar - Awards

List of Awards

These are the awards that he has won throughout his musical career, it isn’t all of the awards he’s ever won because if that were the case then this would be a long list however, this contains all of the important awards he has ever won. It will contain awards from multiple organizations such as The Grammys, The BET Awards, The MTV Awards, and finally, his Pulitzer Prize.

Pulizter Prize:

Awarded the 2018 Pulitzer Prize in Music for his 4th studio album DAMN.
