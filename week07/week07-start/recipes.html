Bacon Egg & Cheese Sandwich:

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 Slices Of Bacon
  • Mozerella Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
Procedure:
  1. Add 2 Eggs Into A Small Bowl, Add Salt & Pepper & Mix Until Fully Blended
  2. Smear Butter Over One Side Of Sliced Bread
  3. Prep Non Stick Pan
  4. Set Heat To Medium
  5. Place Bacon Onto Pan
  6. Cook Until Crispy (Roughly 10 Minutes)
  7. Remove Bacon & Spread Butter & Then Add Eggs
  8. Place Bread In Pan Butter Side Up
  9. Flip Egg Onto Bread
  10. Add Your Bacon Slices, Cheddar & Mozerella
  11. Cook Until Well Done (5-10 Minutes)
  12. Place On Plate & Enjoy

Yellow Rice:

Ingredients:
  • 2 Cups Of Rice
  • 1 Liter/4 Cups Of Chicken Broth
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves Of Garlic
  • 1 Medium Tomato
  • Parsley Or Cilantro
  • 1 Teaspoon Of Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Of Curry Powder
Procedure:
  1. Chop Onions Into Thin Slices
  2. Throw Chopped Onions Into Pan, Stir & Let Cook For A Couple Of Minutes
  3. While Onion Cooks, Chop Up A Small Bunch Of Parsley (You Can Use Cilantro Also Or Both If Preffered)
  4. Add Parsley To Onions, Mix, Let It Cook For A Couple Of Minutes
  5. Meanwhile, Mince 2 Cloves Of Garlice
  6. Add Minced Garlic To Pan, Cook For 30 Seconds, Stirring Constantly
  7. Next Add 2 Cups of Dried Rice (If Preffered Fluffy Rice, Rinse Before Cooking)
  8. Chop Up Medium Tomato Into Cubes
  9. Add Tomatoes & Stir
  10. Add 1 Liter (4 Cups) Of Chicken Broth
  11. Let Broth Boil On Hot Heat
  12. Add 1 Teaspoon Of Salt
  13. Add 1/2 Teapsoon Of Curry Powder Then Mix
  14. As Water Boils Down & Barely Skims Top Of Rice, Reduce Heat To Very Low & Cover for 15 Minutes
  15. After 15 Minutes Are Up, Fluff The Rice Around With Spoon Or Spatula
  16. Place On Plate & Enjoy

Honey Garlic Chicken Dinner:

Ingredients:
  • 3/4 Pound (340g) Of Chicekn Breast, Boneless & Skinless
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons Of Flour (18g)
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil (13g)
  • 2 Tablespoons Of Unsalted Butter (28g)
  • 2 Cloves Of Garlic (12g)
  • 1 Tablespoon Of Light Soy Sauce (16g)
  • 1 Tablespoon Of White Vinegar (14g)
  • 2 & 1/2 Tablespoon Of Honey (53g)
Procedure:
  1. Cut 3/4 Pound Chicken Breast / Boneless / Skinless Into Cubes
  2. Add Salt & Crushed Black Pepper Then Mix
  3. Add 2 Tablespoons Of Flour
  4. Mix Until Chicken Is Fully Coated
  5. Mince 2 Gloves Of Garlic
  6. Add 1 Tablespoon Of Light Soy Sauce Into Seperate Cup
  7. Add 1 Tablesppon Of Light Vinegar
  8. Add 2 & 1/2 Tablespoon Of Honey & Mix
  9. Add 1 Tablespoon Of Olive Oil Into Pan
  10. Throw 1 Tablespoon Of Butter
  11. Add Your Chicken To The Pan
  12. Cook Until All Sides Are Golden (Takes Roughly 5-7 Minutes)
  13. Cook Them One Side At A Time, Do Not Frequently Turn Them
  14. Move Chicken To Edges
  15. Add 1 Tablespoon Of Butter % Minced Garlic You Chopped From Before Into Pan And Saute For 30 Seconds Until Fragrant
  16. Add Honey Sauce Prepared From Earlier
  17. Turn Heat To Medium-Low & Coat Chicken With Sauce
  18. Place On Plate & Enjoy
